Standard Visit Protocols

Every assigned visit consists of the following standardized actions:

  • Access: Retrieve keys and verify entry code.
  • Hydration/Feeding: Sanitize bowls and provide fresh supplies.
  • Wellness: Visual health inspection of the pet.
  • Exercise: 20-minute physical activity session.
  • Security: Lock all doors and return keys to secure lockbox.
