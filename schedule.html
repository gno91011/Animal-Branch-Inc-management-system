Daily Schedule and Visit Requirements

Standard Visit Protocol

To ensure consistent care, every visit must include:

  1. Home Entry: Retrieve keys from lockbox using secure code.
  2. Hydration & Feeding: Clean bowls and provide fresh water/food.
  3. Health Monitor: Check for signs of illness or stress.
  4. Exercise: Perform minimum 20-minute physical activity.
  5. Departure: Confirm all appliances are off and doors locked.
